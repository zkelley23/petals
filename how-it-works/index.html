How Petals works

Every month, we create unique arrangements based on the season. For example, in the summer you’ll receive a bright, summery arrangement and in the winter you’ll receive a regal, snowy arrangement.

We have a designated delivery date each month based on location. Your arrangement will be delivered to your door. On vacation? Let us know through the Petals app, and we’ll deliver your arrangement before or after your trip.

Use the Petals app to tell us when your special dates are, such as birthdays and anniversaries. We’ll make a special delivery with an appropriately themed arrangement on the date.

As a subscriber to the Basic++ plan you can create a custom arrangement twice per year. It’s super easy to create your arrangement with the Petals app.
